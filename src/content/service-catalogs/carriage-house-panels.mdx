A Carriage House Panel garage door is a **distinctive design** characterized by elongated, evenly spaced vertical grooves or panels running the full height of the door. This style offers a sleek, modern appearance while adding significant texture and depth to your home's exterior surface. 

### Why Choose Carriage House Panels?
This style seamlessly combines modern aesthetics with functional appeal, making it a popular choice across a variety of architectural styles, from traditional to contemporary.

* **Durable Construction:** Features a heavy-duty steel skin for long-lasting performance.
* **Superior Insulation:** Built with an **R-Value 18** and a **2-inch door thickness** to ensure maximum energy efficiency in the GTA climate.
* **Customizable Details:** While decorative hardware is sold separately, these doors are designed to be tailored to your home's unique look.
