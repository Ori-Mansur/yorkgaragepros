## The York Garage Pros Installation Process

Our installation service is comprehensive, ensuring your door operates silently and safely for years to come:

1.  **Professional Measurement**: We ensure a perfect fit to prevent air gaps.
2.  **Full System Replacement**: We don't just swap the door; we install new heavy-duty tracks, high-cycle springs, and quiet rollers.
3.  **Insulation Focus**: We offer doors with up to **R-18 insulation** to protect your home's thermal envelope.
4.  **Smart Opener Integration**: Complete your setup with a Wi-Fi enabled LiftMaster or Chamberlain opener.
